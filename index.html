/ FAQ /

Answers, no fluff.

What is 11agents.ai exactly?

A swarm of 11 specialised AI agents, each running one growth channel for your business — X, Reddit, SEO, GEO, TikTok, KOC, KOL, MCP, Ads, owned channels, and EDM. You hire them once for $999/year. They publish GTM artifacts every week. Every artifact is reviewed before it ships.

What exactly do I get for $999?

One lobster-box hardware unit, etched with your founders' edition number. The full 11-agent swarm working for your business for 365 days from activation. Weekly published artifacts across all 11 channels. Iron Triangle review on every output. No subscription. No metered usage. No upsell.

When does the Kickstarter campaign go live?

Q3 2026 (July – September). The early-bird $799 tier is limited to the first 250 backers. Sign up now and you'll get the campaign link the moment it goes live — so you can grab early-bird pricing before it sells out.

What counts as a "shipped GTM artifact"?

Anything published to its destination platform with a public URL. An X post is shipped when it's live on X. An SEO article is shipped when it's indexed. A KOL partnership is shipped when the contract is signed. We don't bill for drafts — only for outputs that hit the world.

Is the hardware actually required?

For the Founders' Edition, yes — the box is your activation key. We're keeping the first year hardware-gated to keep the cohort small (limited to 1,000 founders). Software-only access opens 2027 with the Open Platform.

Who reviews the agent outputs before they ship?

Every Iron Triangle has a Reviewer slot. For your founders' year, that's a real human on our team holding the bar against the channel's rubric. Future versions of the platform will let you appoint your own Reviewers — including yourself.

What about my IP and brand voice?

You retain full ownership of everything we produce on your behalf. We train the agents on your existing voice (one onboarding call, a couple of past posts, your brand guide if you have one) and they ship in your voice forever after.

What's the refund policy?

30 days from your activation date, no questions asked. We refund the full $999 (including shipping). After 30 days you can still cancel any future renewal — we just won't pro-rate the current year.
